## 3. Core Capabilities & Value Drivers

### 3.1 Predictive Hiring Intelligence
Stop guessing. Our proprietary AI weights candidates based on four critical dimensions:
- **Skill Match**: Exact alignment with technical and soft skills required.
- **Experience Depth**: Contextual understanding of their past roles, not just keyword matching.
- **Evidence Level**: Verification of impact (e.g., "Led a team of 10" vs. "Good leadership").
- **Culture Signals**: Alignment with your organizational values based on semantic analysis.

### 3.2 Automated Pre-Screening (AI Copilot)
Instead of scheduling 30-minute phone screens for every candidate, our **AI Technical Evaluator** engages candidates via chat immediately upon application. It asks tailored questions based on their resume and the job description, transcribing and summarizing the results for your team.

### 3.3 Bulletproof Compliance (DPDP Ready)
Data privacy is non-negotiable. reckruit.ai is built with a strictly isolated multi-tenant architecture. 
- **Right to be Forgotten**: One-click deletion of candidate data.
- **Audit Trails**: Immutable logs of who viewed, approved, or rejected a candidate.
- **Bias Scanning**: The platform actively scans your Job Descriptions for non-inclusive language before they are published to the external portal.

## 4. Implementation & Co-Existence Strategy

You do not need to replace your current ATS to get the benefits of AI. reckruit.ai is designed to operate as an **intelligence layer**:

1. **Phase 1 (Ingestion)**: We connect to your career page or existing ATS. Candidates flow into our semantic matching engine.
2. **Phase 2 (Orchestration)**: Our AI scores, ranks, and conducts initial chat screenings.
3. **Phase 3 (Synchronization)**: Only the top 10% of highly qualified, pre-screened candidates are pushed back into your core ATS (e.g., Darwinbox) for final interviews and offer generation.

Implementing reckruit.ai yields immediate, measurable returns:
