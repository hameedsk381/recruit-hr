# Pitch Script: reckruit.ai

**Target Audience:** VP of HR, Chief Human Resources Officer (CHRO), or Talent Acquisition Directors.
**Duration:** ~5-7 Minutes
**Tone:** Professional, authoritative, empathetic to recruiter pain points.

---

## 1. The Hook (0:00 - 1:00)
*Goal: Grab attention by stating a problem they know they have, but haven't solved.*

"Hi [Client Name], thank you for taking the time today. 

I want to start with a question: **How much of your talent team’s week is spent actually talking to great candidates, versus just sifting through resumes that don't match the job?**

If your team is like most enterprise HR departments, the answer is probably 70% screening, 30% recruiting. You're paying highly skilled talent professionals to do data entry and keyword matching. 

Worse, when you get 500 applicants for a role, human fatigue sets in. Top tier talent slips through the cracks, unconscious bias creeps in, and candidates drop out because it takes three weeks to hear back.

That’s why we built **reckruit.ai**."

---

## 2. The Solution & Co-Existence (1:00 - 2:30)
*Goal: Introduce the product and immediately defuse the "we don't want to migrate systems" objection.*

"reckruit.ai is an **Active Intelligence Orchestration Platform**. It replaces manual screening with predictive AI. 

Now, before we go further, let me address the elephant in the room: **We are not here to rip and replace your existing ATS.** We know migrations are painful. 

Instead, think of us as an intelligence layer that sits *on top* of your current infrastructure—whether that’s Darwinbox, Zoho, or Greenhouse. We catch the thousands of inbound applications, use our AI to do the heavy lifting, and then push only the top 5% of highly-qualified, pre-screened candidates directly into your core ATS for final interviews."

---

## 3. The Three Pillars of Value (2:30 - 5:00)
*Goal: Showcase the core features framed strictly as business benefits.*

"We deliver value across three specific pillars:

**Pillar 1: The Match-First Engine**
Instead of recruiters spending hours reading resumes, our AI reads, understands, and scores hundreds of resumes against your specific job description in seconds. It doesn't just look for keywords; it analyzes Skill Match, Experience Depth, Evidence of Impact, and Culture Signals. Your recruiters log in and immediately see a ranked shortlist.

**Pillar 2: The AI Technical Evaluator**
We don't stop at the resume. When a candidate applies through our portal, they don't go into a black hole. They are immediately engaged by our AI Copilot for a short chat-based screening. The AI asks tailored questions based on their resume to validate their skills. You get a fully transcribed scorecard before a human recruiter even picks up the phone.

**Pillar 3: Bulletproof Compliance & Blind Screening**
We know data privacy and diversity are top priorities. Our platform is fully compliant with the DPDP act. More importantly, we offer configurable **Blind Screening**. Our AI automatically redacts names, photos, emails, and nationalities from profiles before the hiring manager sees them, ensuring that your initial screening is 100% objective and skills-based."

---

## 4. The Ask / Call to Action (5:00 - 6:00)
*Goal: Close the pitch and move to the next step.*

"In short, reckruit.ai shifts your team from *administrators* to *closers*. We reduce time-to-hire, eliminate unconscious bias, and provide a premium, transparent experience for your candidates.

I’d love to show you a quick 5-minute live demo of how our Match-First Engine processes a batch of 50 resumes instantly. Does that sound like something that would be valuable for your team to see?"

---

### *Objection Handling Cheat Sheet*

*   **"We already use AI in our ATS."**
    *   *Response:* "Most legacy ATS platforms use basic keyword parsers and call it AI. We use advanced semantic models that understand the *context* of experience, not just ctrl+f matching. Plus, our AI actively conducts chat screenings."
*   **"Is this safe for candidate data?"**
    *   *Response:* "Absolutely. We employ strict tenant isolation, we do not use your candidate data to train external models, and our architecture supports instantaneous 'Right to be Forgotten' requests to comply with DPDP and GDPR."
*   **"Candidates won't want to chat with an AI."**
    *   *Response:* "Data shows candidates prefer a 5-minute AI chat that gets them immediate feedback over waiting 4 weeks in silence. It drastically improves the employer brand."
