Job Description (JD) Validation Criteria

Purpose:
To ensure every Job Description (JD) is complete, structured, and ready for AI-based resume matching or ATS processing.

JD Validation Summary
A validated JD must clearly answer the following:
1. What is the role? (Title, Summary)
2. What skills are required? (Key Skills)
3. What will the candidate do? (Responsibilities)
4. What level and qualification are needed? (Experience, Education)
5. Where and how will they work? (Location, Employment Type)

If any of these areas are incomplete or unclear, the JD should be marked 'Invalid' or 'Needs Revision' before processing.

JD Validation Outcome

Note:
For high-quality matches, JDs should be:
• Clear, structured, and keyword-rich.
• Free from vague or creative job titles.
• Written in bullet points rather than long paragraphs.
